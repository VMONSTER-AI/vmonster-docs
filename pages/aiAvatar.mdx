---
title: "AI Avatar"
---

The AI Avatar is created within the [VMONSTER Studio](https://app.vmonster.io) designed to interact seamlessly with users.

You can develop a variety of AI Avatars to cater to diverse user needs.

## Key Features

---

### Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Employs TTS technology to convert text input into natural-sounding speech, enhancing user engagement.

### Lipsync Technology

Integrates lipsync technology to synchronize the Agent's lip movements with speech in real-time, creating a more realistic and immersive experience.
